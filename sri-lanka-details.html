Sri Lanka — Island of Teas, Beaches & Wildlife

Sri Lanka is a tropical island nation known for its golden beaches, lush tea plantations, ancient temples, and diverse wildlife. From the cultural riches of Kandy and Galle to the safari parks of Yala and the surf of Arugam Bay, Sri Lanka offers a rich blend of nature, history, and cuisine for every traveler.

Explore colonial forts, climb through misty tea estates, discover colorful markets, and spot elephants and leopards on guided safaris. Friendly locals, vibrant festivals, and a compact island layout make it easy to experience many highlights in a single trip.
