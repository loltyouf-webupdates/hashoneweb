Overview

Discover the true essence of Sri Lanka on this unforgettable 6 Nights / 7 Days journey, blending culture, nature, wildlife, and beach relaxation. From ancient kingdoms and sacred cities to misty hill country train rides, thrilling safaris, and golden southern beaches, this tour offers a perfectly balanced Sri Lankan experience designed for comfort, discovery, and adventure.

Includeds

This package includes airport pick-up and drop-off, accommodation in carefully selected hotels, daily breakfast, private air-conditioned transportation with an experienced driver-guide, sightseeing tours as per the itinerary, a scenic hill-country train journey (subject to availability), Yala National Park safari including jeep and entrance fees, and all applicable government taxes.

  • Airport pick-up & drop-off.
  • Accommodation in selected hotels.
  • Daily breakfast.
  • Private air-conditioned vehicle with professional driver-guide.
  • All sightseeing as per itinerary.
  • Hill country scenic train experience (subject to availability).
  • Yala National Park safari (jeep + entrance).
  • All government taxes.

Top Highlights

Experience the best of Sri Lanka by exploring ancient heritage sites such as Sigiriya, Anuradhapura, Polonnaruwa, and Kandy, enjoying breathtaking hill-country views in Nuwara Eliya and Ella with the iconic Nine Arch Bridge, embarking on an exciting wildlife safari in Yala National Park, and unwinding on the beautiful southern beaches including Mirissa, perfectly blending culture, adventure, nature, and relaxation in one unforgettable journey.

  • Explore Sigiriya Rock Fortress & ancient heritage cities

  • Scenic Ella train journey & Nine Arch Bridge experience
  • Cool climate & tea plantations of Nuwara Eliya
  • Exciting wildlife encounters at Yala Safari

  • Perfect mix of culture, adventure & beach leisure

Itinerary

This 13 Nights / 14 Days itinerary takes you on a complete journey across Sri Lanka, beginning in Negombo and continuing through the ancient cultural cities of Anuradhapura, Sigiriya, and Polonnaruwa, followed by the sacred city of Kandy. The tour then moves into the scenic hill country of Nuwara Eliya and Ella, featuring breathtaking landscapes and a memorable train experience, before heading to Yala for an exciting wildlife safari. The journey concludes with relaxation along the stunning southern coastline, including Mirissa and a peaceful beach stay, offering the perfect balance of culture, nature, adventure, and leisure.
